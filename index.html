Company Profile


TechnoSoft Services, is an ISO 9001-2008 certified company, a visionary organization, offering IT consulting services since 1998, has carried out the pioneering work, at time IT was in its nascent stage, by deploying the computer technology to several industries. Turn key computerization projects and enterprise wide software solutions implementation amounting to nearly 1000 man years of work done, TechnoSoft Services is well poised to offer specialized software solutions and system administration services to its clients. Competence built in through training and development of its human resources over two decades, has enabled TechnoSoft Services to offer e-learning solutions and Learning Management Systems. The critical gap between specific skills needed and in house resources is filled by TechnoSoft Services through its staff augmentation along with lateral hiring services and human resource consulting.

About Us



History


The company was formed in the year 1998 catering to the IT manpower needs of Tata Iron And Steel Company in Jamshedpur. With a humble beginning of just a handful of people the company has grown into one of the prominent manpower facility providers to the Tata steel company in the last 11 odd years. We strongly believe that it all happened due to the sheer hard work of all our employees who exceeded all expectations of the customers and earned us the rapport that we have with the company today.



Strength


The strength of the company lies in the team work of 120 employees. We have ensured inculcation of values that promote collective efforts and strongly promote creativity at all levels. Strong belief that team work works the best gave us an opportunity to create this wonderful team which at all given time works in unison to enhance the value of the company.



Vision


It is our dream to be a strong 500 employee company by the year 2015 providing manpower and solutions to at least 5 large corporations.



Guiding Philosophy


Create Value for our customers in everything that you do. This is what we inculcate in the heart of each employee to ensure single minded focus on our goal..
